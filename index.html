TARTANHACKS is the largest Hackathon in Pittsburgh! Organized by ScottyLabs, it's a 24-hour hackathon where participants from all over the country create innovative projects. Come on over to hack, learn from our workshops and incredible speakers, and meet other hackers.

This year's theme is...

MOSAIC

For 24 hours, step into a world built from fragments,
floating tiles, ideas that refract and snap into place.

Nothing arrives whole; everything begins as a piece. You and your team gather scattered bits of vision, skill, and code, assembling them into something greater than any single part. Every insight becomes a tile and every teammate a new color. As the hours pass, the picture will sharpen, a Mosaic taking shape.

Enter with fragments.
Leave with something whole.
